About Us

The World's Danze Music Zone in cyberspace, iDMZ Sayaw Pinoy!, was launched in August 2011 as a tribute to the iconic 89.1 DMZ radio station that had been a part of Filipino dance music culture until 2001. It gained popularity as the first and only non-stop dance music platform accessible worldwide through live streaming on www.89dmz.com and www.idmzsayawpinoy.com.

Just two weeks after its launch, iDMZ experienced a significant increase in web statistics, with a tenfold rise in hits. The iDMZ Facebook group, named 89DMZ Danze Music Zone, brought together Filipinos from all over the world, fostering connections through chat and music mix sharing. Leveraging the enthusiasm on Facebook, iDMZ introduced the "BE HEARD Worldwide" program, providing a platform for followers and members to showcase their DJ skills and music mixing talents. This program airs every Friday.
